Our Story

Not just a café.
A point of view.

Noir & Grounds was born from a simple obsession: great coffee deserves a great room. We sourced beans from three continents, trained our baristas like chefs, and designed a space that feels like staying in rather than going out.

Every dish on our menu is made from scratch. Every coffee is dialled in daily. Every corner of this café was chosen deliberately — because details matter, and you can taste the difference.
